Travel App

This site will be a travel application. The main use case is the finance module, where users can add deposits, exchanges, and purchases to the database, and then can view their purchase list and total funds.

Further out, the site can be extended to include graphs of some of the data, for example in order to see which country is the cheapest.

Additionally, the site should include a way for users to write accounts of their trip and to upload pictures.
